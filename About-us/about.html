About Our Company

Carline Auto Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 1964 in Pune, India, Carline Auto stands as a beacon of engineering excellence and technological innovation in the automotive sector.

Founded by visionary leadership, our journey began with a modest 15 employees and a 5000 sq. feet workshop. Today, we take pride in our vibrant team of over 800 dedicated professionals, operating across nine factories.

Spanning nine state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities across Pune, we leverage a massive 400,000 sq. feet of manufacturing space to deliver unparalleled quality. Our portfolio encompasses automotive components, precision engineering, and innovative automotive solutions.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional automotive solutions through innovation, quality, and customer-centric approaches.
