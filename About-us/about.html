About Us

Welcome To Carline Auto Pvt. Ltd

Carline Auto Private Limited (CAPL) was incorporated in 1989. The Chairman and Managing Director of CAPL, Late Shri. Ashok Sawant, a Technocrat, successful for 30 years in supplying key Sheet metal Components and subassemblies to M/s Tata Motors Ltd, Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd., Matsushita, Hatz Germany. The company has earned plaudits for developing proprietary sheet metal components such as Air Filters (required for Telco 407 vehicles & Exports), Air Cylinders (for Hydraulic brake System), Air Tanks, Sensing Tanks, Deep drawn components such as Shell Fuel Filter, Lubricating Oil Filter.

The Chairman and Managing Director of the company is Late Mr. Ashok J. Sawant, a Technocrat with extensive experience in Automobile Industry. Mr. Sawant was earlier associated with M/s Telco for more than a decade and was working in Production Engineering Division when he left Telco to start Carline Pressings Private Limited. With a dedicated workforce of 35 employees, 4 supervisors in production, and 5 office staff members, we ensure quality and efficiency in every project we undertake.
