About Carline Auto

Pioneers in precision sheet-metal manufacturing since 1989

Our Legacy

Carline Auto Private Limited (CAPL) was incorporated in 1989 under the visionary leadership of Late Shri. Ashok Sawant, a renowned technocrat with over three decades of excellence in supplying critical sheet-metal components to industry giants including Tata Motors Ltd., Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd., Matsushita, and Hatz Germany.

We have earned recognition for developing proprietary components such as Air Filters (for Telco 407 vehicles & exports), Air Cylinders for hydraulic brake systems, Air Tanks, Sensing Tanks, and deep-drawn parts like Shell Fuel Filters and Lubricating Oil Filters.

"From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted partner of India's leading automotive brands — built on precision, quality, and trust."

-Shri. Ashok J. Sawant, Founder
